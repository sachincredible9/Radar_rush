PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: May 2024

Your privacy is paramount. Radar Rush is designed with a "Privacy First" approach. Below is a summary of how we handle your information.

1. Information Collection

If you choose to use Google Login or Sign in with Apple, we collect your email address and basic profile information solely for the purpose of syncing your game progress and milestones across devices.

2. Data Usage

We use the collected data to provide account management, prevent cheating, and potentially offer personalized in-app purchase options in the future. We do NOT sell your data to third parties.

3. Analytics

We use anonymized telemetry (e.g., high scores, levels reached) to improve game difficulty scaling and performance. No personally identifiable information is linked to these metrics.

4. Security

All user data is stored securely via Google Firebase with industry-standard encryption.

5. Contact

For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at support@radarrush.com
